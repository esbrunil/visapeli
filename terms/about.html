Visapeli

Visapeli is an incredible experience in which you will dive into a world of information and knowledge. After dedicating countless hours into this masterpiece of educational design, you may finally achieve the pure respect among your friends and peers alike. Usually after blurting out random pieces of pocket-information at convenient times or simply destroying your friendgroup in the yearly Trivial Pursuit run in your favourite bar.
